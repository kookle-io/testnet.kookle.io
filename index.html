About us

Kookle is lead by a group of developers and technology professionals who are passionate about the potential of decentralized applications. The Kookle Oracle is on the block chain using smart contracts designed to deliver data in a way that can revolutionize how industries settle payments ranging from Hospitality to Financial and Medical services.

Projects built on the foundation of decentralization and disintermediation are the untamed wilderness of our future. As we move further away from rigid financial structures and traditional mindsets, we become free to discover new ways to solve problems and relate to one another. We are developing the Kookle Oracle to help tear down long-established ideals and reinventing new more efficient ways of doing things.

The Kookle Oracle will offer an alternative to the permission-less community of data providers, validators, and token holders that harvest “crypto secure data” from one or more sources and putting that information on-chain for subscribers benefit.

The strengths our team brings to Kookle, include over 10 years’ experience developing software applications to become a leading services provider to the hospitality and services industry in Thailand and throughout South East Asia.
